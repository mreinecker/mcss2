If the other driver, a defective product or the commercial property owner’s negligence caused your injuries, you may seek compensation for your pain and suffering and disability.

Only around 4% of New Jersey Attorneys have been so honored in any given year.

Worker's Compensation

The New Jersey Worker’s Compensation Act, if you are injured on the job you have the right to medical treatment and temporary disability benefits while you are out of work paid for by the employer’s Worker’s Compensation carrier.

While you need to request authorization for treatment, insurance carriers all too often now have only two purposes in mind: stop you from getting legitimate treatment and return you to work no matter what so they can stop paying benefits. Also, some employers try to deny that you were injured on the job to avoid making a claim. But you do not have to take their answer as the final say. Kevin Kovacs has been helping injured workers for over 30 years obtain the benefits they are entitled to receive, and going to Court if necessary to get an Order to Compel employers and insurance carriers to abide by the Law and provide those benefits, at no cost to you.

Also, if you are injured on the job, you have the right to obtain a monetary award to compensate you for the extent of the permanent disability you have suffered. Kevin will use his skill and experience to get you the best result and if your case cannot settle for fair value he is prepared to take it to Trial. The award he obtains for you will help offset the losses you suffered and will also serve to extend the time when you can seek further treatment if your condition gets worse. The cost to you is nothing up front and nothing if Kevin is not successful on your behalf. The Court will award Kevin fees representing a small portion of the money he obtains for you.

So if you are injured on the job and need help navigating the complex and all too often frustrating system, call Kevin at (908) 526-7111 or e-mail him at Kovacslawoffice@gmail.com
